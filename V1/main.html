Theremin with Leap Motion

About Leap Motion Theremin

A theremin is a musical instrument that is played without touching it. It detects the distance between the player's hands and the instrument's two antennae, which controls the pitch and volume of the sound.

Leap Motion is a gesture-based control technology for digital devices. The Leap Motion sensor is utilized in the theremin project to track the player's hand motions and translate them into the sounds produced by the theremin.

This results in a one-of-a-kind and immersive musical experience, allowing the player to operate the instrument in a more intuitive and natural manner.
